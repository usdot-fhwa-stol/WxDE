Clarus Observations — ARCHIVED!

The University of North Dakota Surface Transportation Weather Research Center has established an active archive of the Clarus observations with their associated metadata and quality checking flags.

The Clarus archived data can be found at http://stwrc.und.edu/undclarus.php with instructions provided.

The files are compressed (gzipped) and contain an extension of tar.gz. Open source software is available at http://www.7-zip.org to extract the compressed files.



We would like to hear from you. If you have a success story using the Clarus System information and want to share your success with others, contact the Clarus System Administrator with the good news.
